THE NIGHT REALM

A World Without Sunlight

The underworld of Hungry Night is not loud or chaotic.
It’s calm, controlled, and unsettling in its beauty.

Moonlight reflects off stone and silk-like webs.
Wings stir the air. Shadows move slowly, as if watching.

This world is designed to feel immersive — a place you don’t rush through, but explore carefully.

THE QUEENS

Two Rulers. One Endless Night.

At the heart of the realm stand two opposing forces.

  • The Queen of Bats

    swift, silent, and precise. Her domain is motion, flight, and sudden shifts.

  • The Queen of Spiders

    patient, calculated, and unyielding. Her power lies in control and inevitability.
